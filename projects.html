Heroes Always Die

This is a 2d platformer shooter game implemented using sfml (simple fast multimedia library). The goal of the game is pretty much to kill all enemies which are alien-like creatures that have the options of either jumping, shooting a projectile, or moving left or right.It has platformer collision detection(platforms and blocks) and has multiple types of weapons (eg. guns, snipers, rocket launchers , grenades , and a healing staff).Each of which has its own benefits and the healing staff heals everyone in the camera range and not only the player.

Flying Undersized Controlled Killer

Github: Flying Undersized Controlled Killer

This is a 3d helicopter assult that implements a fly instead of a helicopter.It uses the opengl library and implements heirarchical behavior.The game includes movement along all axes as well as camera control in those axes.It also has 3 different weapon types(Player's rocket like projectile , spider's web , and a draggable object that can be dropped on enemies). There are also 3 enemy types (humans , dragonflies , spiders). The game include particle effects for the weapon types as well as death particle animations for the enemies in addition to splines for defining the web's shape.The objective is also to kill all the enemies in the room-like map.
